What is the fundamental difference between an affordance ([[affordance]]) and a signifier ([[signifier]]) according to Don Norman?

### Answers

A. An affordance is a digital component only, while a signifier exists solely in the physical world.

B. An affordance represents the physical or functional action possibility, while a signifier is the cue that signals the existence of this action and guides towards it.

C. An affordance is a product of aesthetic visual design, while a signifier represents the system's development speed and code.

D. An affordance is determined exclusively by the end user, while a signifier is defined by the product manager.

Correct: B
Explanation: אפורדנס הוא עצם קיומה של אפשרות פעולה (למשל, הדלת פיזית ניתנת לדחיפה או כפתור דיגיטלי ניתן ללחיצה), בעוד שסיגניפייר הוא הרמז או הסימן החזותי המבהיר למשתמש שהאפשרות הזו קיימת ומכוון אותו לפעול (למשל, המילה "דחף" או כפתור מוגבה עם צל).
Learning Objective: Define affordance and signifier and distinguish between them.
Misconception: Students often confuse the two, using the term 'affordance' to describe a visual cue (which is actually a signifier).

How would you classify the design failure occurring in the following situation?

### Scenario

A user arrives at the entrance of a new office. A long, prominent metal handle is mounted on the door, inviting grasping and pulling. The user grabs the handle and pulls it toward their body, but the door does not open. Only after noticing a small sign that says 'Push' do they realize they need to push the door to enter.

### Answers

A. The situation presents a hidden affordance (Hidden Affordance).

B. The situation presents a perfect natural mapping (Natural Mapping).

C. The situation presents a false/misleading signifier (False/Misleading Signifier).

D. The situation presents an absolute physical constraint (Physical Constraint).

Correct: C
Explanation: ידית האחיזה הבולטת משמשת כסיגניפייר חזותי למשיכה (Pull). אולם, האפורדנס האמיתי של הדלת הוא דחיפה (Push). מאחר שהרמז החזותי (הידית) סותר את הפעולה האמיתית האפשרית, מדובר בסיגניפייר כוזב או מטעה (מצב המכונה "דלת נורמן").
Learning Objective: Identify design failures such as false affordances, hidden affordances, or misleading signifiers.
Misconception: Students might think it is a hidden affordance, but the ability to push is not physically hidden; it is simply cued incorrectly by the wrong handle.

Which of the following best describes the principle of mapping (Mapping) designed in the most natural and intuitive way?

### Answers

A. A car steering wheel which, when turned left, turns the wheels left.

B. A home light switch panel where the leftmost switch turns on the lamp in the rightmost room.

C. An elevator where the ground floor button is placed at the top of the vertical panel, and the rooftop floor button is at the bottom.

D. A TV remote control where the volume up button is located on the back of the remote.

Correct: A
Explanation: מיפוי טבעי (Natural Mapping) נשען על התאמה פיזית או מרחבית ברורה וצפויה בין הבקר לבין האלמנט הנשלט. סיבוב ההגה שמאלה שמוביל לפנייה שמאלה הוא קשר טבעי ומיידי. המקרים האחרים מציגים מיפוי גרוע, הפוך או שרירותי המאלץ מאמץ קוגניטיבי.
Learning Objective: Explain and apply the mapping principle (Mapping) in interfaces.
Misconception: Students sometimes struggle to understand that mapping is not about text or action description (signifier), but about the physical spatial and positional relationship of the controls.
